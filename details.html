Nyoka

Nyoka was found and rescued in a dark jungle near the capital of the Democratic Republic of Congo. Her rescuer found her as an infant an named her Nyoka which means snake in the congolese bantu language of lingala. While being extremely venus is very playful, fast, and loves to eat rabbits

Date of Rescue: 05/25/2023

Gender: Female

Food: Birds, Rodents, Bats, and smaller snakes
